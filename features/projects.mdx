## Getting Started with Projects

To create and manage a project:
1. Go to the **"Projects"** tab in your IntualAI dashboard.
2. Click on **"Create Project"** and provide a name and description.
3. Add team members, assign roles, and configure project-specific settings.

---

## Advanced Features

- Granular access control for team members.
- Version control for workflows and datasets.
- Project-specific billing and resource tracking.
